# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect respectful, professional collaboration focused on technical outcomes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, doxxing, threats, and bad-faith disruption.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to: `ogsecs@gmail.com`
